LD 50 - Inevitable Dungeon

For lundum dare 50 the theme was 'Inevitable' for this jam I paired up with Mista Giggles. We worked on a game called 'Inevitable Dungeon' a small 2D pixel art dungeon crawler where death is Inevitable, but items stored in your death bag can be kept when you respawn.

The game can be played on my ITCH.IO page. This is the version that was submitted at the end of the jam without many additional bug fixes. The game was built using the GODOT Engine using GDSCRIPT. Everything was created by myself and Matt for the jam with no asssets or previous work used.

LD 49 - Scootles

For lundum dare 49 the theme was 'unstable' I teamed up with Mista Giggles and Tom Fox. We worked on a game called scootles which involved a mobility scooter with an unstable throttle. The premise was to collect your shopping without crashing into everything.

I did alot of the custom models for the game. Some assets from the unity asset store were used such as the characters and some of the props were from a Kenny asset pack. We all contributed on the coding side.
